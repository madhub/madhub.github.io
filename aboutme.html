About Me

I am Sr. Software Architect at Philips Healthcare India, leading the activities of creating classic DICOM & DICOM-RS REST services using Docker & Kubernetes technologies. Goal is to create & deploy service on-premise & in the cloud.

Interests
I am interested anything related to technology whether it is Operating Systems, Cloud, new gadgets.
I like to keep up to date with new developments in technology and I am always interested in learning new and better ways of doing things in software development.

My Current interests

Docker & Kubernetes
Virtualization related technologies (Virtual BOX, KVM, and Hyper-V).
Cloud related technologies.
Application of Web sockets for Real-time instrument monitor.
Application of low power wireless technologies such as NFC and Bluetooth LE.

Education
Engineering Degree in Electronics & Communications.
